The Quizmas Elves are a group of 6 developers who have come together to create a festive quiz for all to enjoy. The quiz is a general knowledge quiz with a festive twist. The quiz is made up of 12 questions, one for each day of Christmas. The quiz is a multiple choice quiz with 4 possible answers for each question. The user can choose to play the quiz with or without music. The user can also choose to play the quiz with or without snow. The user can also choose to play the quiz with or without sound effects. The user can also choose to play the quiz with or without a timer. The user can also choose to play the quiz with or without a countdown. The user can also choose to play the quiz with or without a score.
